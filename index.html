Projects

Wheel Loader Simulator

Developed this immersive Wheel Loader Simulator in collaboration with the design, 3D, and analytics teams. The project trains operators of a CAT 966L in a realistic VR environment, focusing on safety and skill-building. It features real-time performance tracking, visual feedback for operational mistakes, and a dynamic weather system (rain, fog, low light) to simulate real-world conditions and challenge operator adaptability.

Rock Breaker Assembly and Disassembly

Developed the Rock Breaker Assembly & Disassembly Simulator in close collaboration with the design, 3D, and analytics teams. This hands-on VR training module guides users through the complete assembly and disassembly of a hydraulic rock breaker, following strict SOPs. It features realistic tool mechanics and real-time analytics that track user accuracy and flag deviations, helping reinforce proper procedures and safe maintenance practices.

Mine Inspection

Designed and developed the Mine Inspection Simulator in collaboration with the 3D, and analytics teams. This VR experience trains minemates to perform underground inspections by observing hazards, answering scenario-based questions, and reporting issues like gas buildup or faulty ventilation to the appropriate authorities. The simulator emphasizes situational awareness, real-time decision-making, and safety compliance, with built-in analytics to assess user understanding and performance.

Dozer Simulator

Developed the Dozer Simulator in collaboration with the design, 3D, and analytics teams. This Screen-Based training module helps operators learn how to doze soil piles and build safety berms, even under challenging weather conditions like rain and fog. The simulator focuses on realistic machine behavior and visibility limitations to improve user skill, safety, and environmental awareness.

Crane Visual Inspection

Developed the Crane Visual Inspection Simulator in collaboration with the design, 3D, and analytics teams. This VR module trains users to perform thorough external and internal inspections of cranes, focusing on issues like rust, cable damage, hydraulic leaks, and system malfunctions. Includes hands-on maintenance tasks such as cleaning the dust bowl, checking for debris in the oil tank, air filter replacement, and greasing. Also features hand signal training for crane operations and analytics-driven assessments to ensure SOP compliance and inspection accuracy.

Includes assessment modules and analytics-driven feedback to evaluate user performance, accuracy, and process understanding.

Vacuum Pump Spindle Maintenance

I contributed to several Training and Assessment modules within the Cleaning & lubrication section.

VCB Maintenance

Developed the VCB (Vacuum Circuit Breaker) Maintenance Simulator in collaboration with the Dev, design, 3D, and analytics teams. This VR module trains users in both operational handling and maintenance procedures of vacuum circuit breakers. It covers safe disassembly, inspection, servicing, reassembly, and includes the full Lockout-Tagout (LOTO) safety process to ensure secure isolation before maintenance. Users are also guided through normal operating sequences to build functional understanding. Assessment modules and analytics monitor procedural accuracy, SOP compliance, and safety adherence.

I contributed to project by making the LOTO process and few modules in operational levels
